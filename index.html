About Me

My road to UX Design has been filled with a lot of twists and turns, but my background in psychology, anthropology, and education has informed my design experience, helping me to build creative and empathetic solutions for users.

UX seeks to provide research that can inform decision making and directly influence the experiences of the everyday user. As a UX Designer, I focus heavily on fully understanding the market, the user, and the goals of the product in order to develop solutions. By emphasizing testing throughout the design process, focusing on familiar UI patterns, and staying true to the initial goals and scope of the project, I ensure that my designs are user-friendly and are creative solutions to real-world problems.
